### 1. NIGHT SHIFT — Nursing RPG
**Status:** v3.0 [14-Patient Expansion]  
**Genre:** Medical Simulation / Survival RPG  

ICU Floor 7. 02:00 hrs. You are the RN on duty. Can you manage 14 complex patients, diagnose critical shifts, and survive until 06:00?
* **Key Features:** Interactive 12-Lead ECG, Med-Math IV Pumps, and Real-time Lab Analysis.
* **Rank System:** Progress from Student Nurse to Chief Nursing Officer.
* **Tech:** HTML5, CSS3 (Neon-Retro UI), Vanilla JavaScript.
